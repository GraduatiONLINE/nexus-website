    Nexus Reality
    Gatherings, reinvented. — Your portal to a nexus of virtual events.
     nexus.virtualevents@gmail.com

our mission

A virtual alternative to physical community events.

Nexus is an immersive, cross-platform, multiplayer, role-playing application framework that provides the users with a virtual community-event experience. With the outbreak of the novel coronavirus, social-distancing regulations have widely been enforced throughout the world. As a result, institutions have been forced to close down their physical establishments, canceling many events their communities have looked forward to attending. Our goal is to provide a virtual alternative to physical location-based events, e.g. the end-of-year graduation ceremony experience for high-school seniors while upholding health regulations and our collective social responsibilities.

More Details

our technology

Stunning, life-like simulations powered by Google Maps & Unity.

Nexus offers a secure, administrator-regulated platform to host customized events based in a three-dimensional world. The application is optimized to perform well across a broad spectrum of devices with enhanced visual fidelity to look as realistic as possible utilizing the Unity3D game engine's High Definition Render Pipeline (HDRP). Our platform is powered by Google Maps to create stunning, life-like representations of real-world locations. With customizable avatars, based in an immersive experience enhanced with a spatial sound system to allow live, realtime voice communication and interaction with other players.

More Details

Multiplayer Capabilities

Nexus allows multiple players to play within a server at once to establish an interactive platform.

Realtime Voice & Video

Our platform is equipped with a spacial sound system to allow live, real voice communication and interaction with other players.

Synced Player Interactions

Our platform allows users to graphically interact with each other through hand-shakes, handing diplomas, etc.

Administrator Regulated

Our platform is regulated by organizers and administrators who can regulate the game experience by altering perspectives, screen content, and room occupation.

Scalable Platform

Nexus ensures a fast, scalable platform through the room system. This allows for large quantities of people to be in-game at the same time without having to render all of them.

Customizable Avatars

Nexus provides customizable avatars with a large selection of customize options to choose from.

Lifelike Graphics

 Our platform is enhanced to look as realistic as possible with new advanced rendering methods developed through the Unity programming platform and realistic building terrain models imported from Google Maps.

Immersive Experience

Nexus places a user in-scene in an exact location in the world replicated through life-like graphics.

Diverse Player Animations

Our platform enables avatars to interact with their surroundings. For example, sitting, clapping, dancing, waving, etc.
