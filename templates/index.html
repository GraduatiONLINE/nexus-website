Our Services


Who we are


Nexus is a cross-platform, multiplayer, location-simulating application that provides the users with a virtual community-event experience. With the outbreak of the novel COVID-19 virus, social-distancing regulations have widely been enforced throughout the world. As a result, institutions have been forced to close down their physical establishments, canceling many events their communities have looked forward to attending.
Visual Facility Design
Audio Facility Design
Construction

Through an online, event-centric, location-simulating, multiplayer role-playing application, our goal is to provide a virtual alternative to physical location-based community events, e.g. the end-of-year graduation ceremony experience for high-school seniors, while upholding health regulations and our collective social responsibilities.
